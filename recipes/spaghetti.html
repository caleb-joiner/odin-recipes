Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is the quintessential Italian pasta. It is long – like a string (hence the name, as spago means string) – round in cross-section and made from durum wheat semolina.

  • Store bought noodles.
  • Store bought sauce.
  • Salt and pepper.
  1. Boil some water.
  2. While waiting for water to boil brown some hamburger meat.
  3. When water comes to a boil throw in them noodles.
  4. Drain grease from meat and set aside.
  5. Once noodles are cooked drain and return to pot.
  6. Add meat and spaghetti sauce to noodles and enjoy!
