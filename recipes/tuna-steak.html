Tuna Steak

Description

Image result for description of a tuna steak Tuna steak is a great alternative to beef steaks (especially when you binge on beef like I do). It's a light, tasty meat that melts in your mouth and it's fishy only in the sense that it tastes an awful lot like beef.

  • 1 tuna steak
  • Lemon pepper
  • Lemon wedge
  1. Season tuna steak with lemon pepper
  2. Cook on medium/high heat for 2-3 minutes each side (add lemon wedge after flipping)
  3. Enjoy a delicious tuna steak!
